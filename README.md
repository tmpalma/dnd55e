## ⚔ What This Mod Does

This mod is a **complete overhaul** of Baldur's Gate 3's ruleset to match the **2024 Player's Handbook (One D&D / 5.5e)**. Every mechanic, class, and feat has been rebuilt from the ground up — not patched on top of the original, but **rewritten**.

> *"The most complete 2024 ruleset overhaul for Baldur's Gate 3."*

## 🧙 Content Overview

### [Classes](./README/README_Class.MD)

Every base class has been **rebuilt from scratch** to match the 2024 PHB — not patched, rebuilt. Three brand new classes have also been added.

- All 14 PHB classes fully updated
- **3 new classes:** Gunslinger · Illrigger · and more
- Subclasses revised and expanded
- Full Extra Attack and multiclassing support

### [Character Backgrounds](./README/README_Background.MD)

All backgrounds updated to the 2024 PHB format — each now grants its **Origin Feat**, two skill proficiencies, and a starting equipment package.

### [Feats](./README/README_Feat.MD)

**80+ feats** spanning five sourcebooks, all revised for the 2024 design philosophy — every feat that grants an ability score increase now does so correctly.

- PHB 2024 feats (Origin, General, Fighting Style, Epic Boon)
- Tasha's Cauldron of Everything feats
- Xanathar's Guide to Everything feats
- The Crooked Moon backgrounds and feats
- Custom supplemental feats

### [Races](./README/README_Race.MD)

All races revised to the 2024 PHB lineage system. New races added:

`Dhampir` · `Kalashtar` · and more — with full trait and passive implementations.

### [Spells](./README/README_Spell.MD)

Hundreds of spells revised to their 2024 versions. Key changes include:

- Concentration removed from several spells (Barkskin, Wrathful Smite, Searing Smite, Spiritual Weapon, Magic Weapon, Stoneskin, Thunderous Smite)
- **Sleep**, **True Strike**, **Vicious Mockery**, **Witch Bolt** fully redesigned
- Summoning spells overhauled (Summon Beast, Summon Fey)
- New spells added: Steel Wind Strike, Mind Sliver, and more

### [Items](./README/README_Item.MD)

Magic items updated and new items added:

`Holy Avenger` · `Moon Sickle` · `Gloves of Heroism` · `Helmet of Smiting` · `Blazer of Benevolence` · `Cap of Curing` · `Chain of Liberation` · `Dragon's Grasp` · `Hellrider's Pride` · `Rupturing Blade` · `Spellthief` · and more.

### Weapon Mastery

The complete **2024 Weapon Mastery** system — all properties implemented:

`Cleave` · `Graze` · `Nick` · `Push` · `Sap` · `Slow` · `Topple` · `Vex`

## ⚙ Installation & Compatibility

- **Install via:** BG3 Mod Manager (in-game)
- **Standalone:** No other mods required
- ⚠ **Not compatible** with saves from before a major version update
- ⚠ **May conflict** with other class or feat overhaul mods
